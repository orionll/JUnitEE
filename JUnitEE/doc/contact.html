Contact

The prefered way to get in contact with anybody responsible for this project is the junitee-user mailing list. Post your problems, wishes, suggestions, ... to this list and we will try to help you as soon as possible.

In case you don't like mailing lists or want to discuss a topic you do not like to share with other subscribers of the list, please send your message to Oliver Rossmueller, who is the current project administrator of JUnitEE.

If you would like to contact the original inventor of this project, please send your message to Jeff Schnitzer.
